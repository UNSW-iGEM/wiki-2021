Betty Skyes



"I represent the Jalanji clan which means I am from the seaside area. My paintings represent stories about the Jalun, sea and everything that lives in and around my traditional home lands. It is important to me to look after the reef so it will be with my family for years to come."
Karrangkal
Acrylic on canvas
61cm x 51cm

I am from the Jalunji clan, Jalunji means seaside. My father used to tell me when we were out fishing not to break the Karrangkal - coral reef or the Jalun - seaside, it will get to rough to hunt and we will have to go back home.
