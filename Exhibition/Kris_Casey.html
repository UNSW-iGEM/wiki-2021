Kris Casey



"The reef for me is a place of endless exploration, unimagined beauty and a thrilling environment in which to be a quest. I feel extremely fortunate to have been able to dive many sections of the Great Barrier Reef from the far northern sections off Cairns to its most southern reach off Lady Elliot Island. Being on the reef always helps me detox from the stress of life on land and reminds me how interconnected and fragile earth's ecosystems are. The aim of my gallery is to showcase the beauty of the reef, from the corals to all the sea critters that call the reef home. It's my greatest hope that we can protect and preserve our reefs so generations to come can enjoy its endless beauty and tranquility."
