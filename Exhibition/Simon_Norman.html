“Recycling those little wires, recycling the ghost net, instead of just throwing it out…. we like to recycle. Everything that they throw at the tip we used to go and collect. The rangers are doing fine bringing in more ghost nets. Especially after monsoon, it’s all over the beach. So we recycle it”
– Sid Bruce Short Joe (Pormpuraaw Art Centre) 2014
