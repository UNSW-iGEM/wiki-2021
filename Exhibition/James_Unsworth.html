James Unsworth



‘I am fortunate enough to live and work on the Great Barrier Reef. In a world that is so media driven it is easy to think there is no hope for our beautiful coral reefs. I hope to show through my photography that the GBR remains a big, beautiful and diverse eco-system. This ecosystem is however experiencing stress and pressure but it certainly isn’t too late to act. These photos show areas that can reseed and repopulate effected reefs providing we protect these incredible ecosystems’
