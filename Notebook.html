This notebook documents the Wet Lab experiments conducted by the 2021 UNSW iGEM team, prior to the Sydney lockdown in late June.

31 May - 4 June


2 June
  • Prepared LB broth and agar
  • Streak plated glycerol stock of E. coli containing HSP22E onto agar to obtain single colonies
3 June
  • Prepared overnight cultures from single colonies of E. coli containing HSP22E
4 June
  • Performed miniprep on E. coli containing HSP22E to extract and purify plasmid for DNA sequencing
  • Conducted restriction digest followed by agarose gel electrophoresis on purified plasmid
  • Streak plated glycerol stock of E. coli containing mCerulean3 onto agar to obtain single colonies

7 June - 11 June

9 June
